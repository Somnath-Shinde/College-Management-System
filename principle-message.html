Principle's Message

Dear students,
It gives me great pleasure to welcome you in this Arts, Commerce and Science College, Hupari, because you are not only going to be a student of this college, but also a member of this family. I congratulate you a lot and welcome you, actually you are mounting on a silver chariot named Arts, Commerce & Science College, Hupari. Due to your success it will glitter more because of you and of course your career also.

The object of this college is to shape your personality and to give direction for your future goals. We are trying our best for all-round development of your personality, for making you progressive. Due to our guidance you will be able to move towards your goals easily and you will certainly achieve them, when you will get your Degree from this college. Mental and Physical fitness is not only enough in today's busy and competitive world but consciousness of social responsibilities is also needed so. psychological and emotional fitness are also important. In unification of these all qualities, this college plays an important role and also increasing you morale, positive attitude, strengthen you to make your existence in this competitive world meaningful.

We emphasize on student- centered, practical-oriented and constructional education for making you strong to face challenges of this competitive and E-world. We provide different platforms for igniting your hidden talent and for maintaining Indian prosperous culture also by arranging cultural programmes and activities. I believe that it will be helpful to you to brighten your future. On behalf of all, my best wishes are always with you.

Dr.S.S.Bhosale


I/C Principal
