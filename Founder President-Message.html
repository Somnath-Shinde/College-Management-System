Founder President's Message

I am very pleased and satisfied because with the blessings of God and with the co-operation from all of you we have started this college successfully in 2019. I also feel proud that this unit has become an excellent example in this Silver City, Hupari. As you know that knowledge is a great wealth as well as power. We have established this unit of Higher Education to facilitate needy and deprived students in this catchment area and make them healthy, wealthy and powerful.

Our teaching as well as Non-teaching staff has contributed their best in the successful beginning of this college. Due to their efforts, gates of higher education are opened for better opportunities. They are doing a great job! Their services are Praiseworthy.

Higher education and Higher Qualifications are master keys to be successful in life. We all are bound to mould students in well cultured citizens. We are committed to serve excellent HE for welfare of society. I appeal all the students to grab this opportunity and make your future very bright. Be ready to face challenges of competitive world. My best wishes are always with you.
