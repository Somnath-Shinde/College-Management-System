Bachelor of Art's

B.A.-I

Group(A):
Compulsory Subjects(two)
1)English for business communication.
2) Marathi or S.T.D.
Group(B):
1)Marathi or English.
2)Economics or hindi
3)History or Psychology
4)Sociology or Political Science
Note:
1) If you select marathi from group(A) you have to select marathi from group(B).
2)select any four subject from group(B).(out of four categories 1,2,3,4 but any one subject from each categories)
B.A.-II

Group(A):
Compulsory Subjects
1)English for business communication.
2)environmental studies. 3)Public Administration.
Group(B):
1)Marathi or History.
2)hindi or Psychology or Political Science
3)English or Economics or Sociology
Note:
1)Student should select any two optional subjects out of above three optional groups which should be out of four optional subjects selected for B.A.-Part I.
2)there are two papers each for optional subjects.
B.A.-III

Group(A):
Compulsory Subjects
1)English
Group(B):
1)Marathi
2)hindi
3)English
4)History
5)Economics
6)Sociology
7) Psychology
8)Political Science
Note:
1)Student should select any one optional subjects for your specialization.
2)there are four papers each for optional subjects.
